Big Dino

I made this around the summer time. I was using my sister's yarn, so when I ran out of the specific green for the head, I used a different but somewhat similar green yarn she had. This was a cute little mess, as the gauge was kept the same for the body, even though the body yarn was finer than the head yarn. However, this didn't help it at all, as now the body almost looks like theres in it as the stitching is more space to the point where gaps are almost everywhere and the stuffing is poking out of it.
